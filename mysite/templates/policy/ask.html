    Instructions
    App Context: Neurolitiks

    Welcome to Neurolitiks, the AI-powered policy analysis tool that helps you evaluate the potential impact and significance of public policies. This app processes public policies using Natural Language Processing (NLP) AI to extract key elements. These elements are then connected with a graph database containing public policies from Mexican states. The policies are rewritten in context, and an AI agent is trained to assess their potential.

    1. Propose a public policy using the form in the left column.
    2. The NLP AI will extract key elements from the policy.
    3. These elements will be connected with a graph database of Mexican state policies.
    4. The policy will be rewritten in context using the database.
    5. An AI agent will evaluate the potential impact and significance of the policy.
    6. View the assessment results in the tables on the right.
    7. Explore relations and insights in the accordion sections.
    8. Use the navigation bar to manage different policies and actions.
    9. Enjoy using Neurolitiks to enhance policy analysis!

    AI Analysis Context

    Discover how we use advanced AI technologies to analyze public policies and evaluate their potential impact. Neurolitiks employs a combination of cutting-edge AI techniques to provide comprehensive insights into policy effectiveness.

    • NLP Analyzer AI: Our Natural Language Processing AI extracts key elements from public policies for further analysis.
    • Graph-Network Analyzer: This AI maps policy elements onto a graph network to identify relationships and patterns.
    • AI Autonomous Agent: An autonomous AI agent evaluates policies' potential based on information and network strength.
